Bootcamp Wellbeing Plan

My wellbeing plan

  • • Go for a hike every weekend

    I want to have some code-free time during the bootcamp, as it is an intensive course. In order to keep my wellbeing in a good condition and not to feel frustrated or too exhuasted.

  • • Stay in touch with friends

    Attending the bootcamp should not take away the social time. I would keep catching up with my friends during bootcamp and talk about how things going here.

  • • Ensure enough time for sleep

    I find myself easily staying up late when I work on coding. Especially when I encounter errors and bugs, I could not go to bed until I get it sorted. I might need to time-box it and leave it and ask for help if it takes too long. Also, I need to set up the bed time. For example, my bed time is 11pm and I stop working at 10.30pm even though I have not get all the work done.
