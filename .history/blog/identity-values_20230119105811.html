2. Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.

In my culture, people tend to be humble, reserved and not express emotions. Since I went overseas, the people I met and the environment have re-shaped my values and identity. Living in a multicultural society, there are heaps of opportunities to experience various cultures, different ways of doing things, different life attitudes and values. I used to be a person who does not know how to express love and hide feelings and emotions all the time. I really appreciated the people I met at my first teaching job. They are the most beautiful people I have met. They showed me how to love and care the people around and made me feel I also have the ability to do the same thing.
